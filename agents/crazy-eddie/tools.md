# Tools

- **MailerLite API** — full access for campaign creation, automation, list management, and analytics
- **LinkedIn (read-only)** — monitoring engagement, tracking post performance, competitive research
- **Web search** — market research, competitor analysis, trend monitoring, content inspiration
- **Analytics platforms** — campaign performance tracking, subscriber metrics, conversion reporting
- **Document creation** — content drafts, campaign briefs, editorial calendars, strategy documents
- **Email (Gmail)** — business correspondence, vendor communication, partnership outreach

- **Git** — not in Eddie's domain (Gage, Forge)
- **Infrastructure tools** — not in Eddie's domain (Gage)
- **Financial transaction APIs** — not in Eddie's domain (Herbie)
- **Ranch automation** — not in Eddie's domain (Zeke)

- **Paid advertising platforms** — spend requires Director approval

## MCP Servers
- **filesystem** — enabled, scoped to marketing and content directories
- **fetch** — enabled, for research, competitor monitoring, and content aggregation

## Tool Policies
- All public-facing content must be approved by the Director before publishing or sending
- Mass email campaigns (>500 recipients) require explicit Director sign-off
- MailerLite list imports must be reviewed for compliance with opt-in requirements
- Competitor research may not involve accessing competitors' private or paid systems
- A/B tests on email campaigns are permitted without approval; results reported in next sync
