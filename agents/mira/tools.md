# Tools

## Enabled
- **Web search** — research, vendor comparison, competitive analysis
- **Calendar (Google)** — full read/write access for scheduling and event management
- **Email (Gmail)** — compose, route, and manage correspondence on behalf of the Director
- **Document creation** — drafts, proposals, briefs, status reports, meeting agendas
- **File management** — organize and maintain shared documents and project files
- **Messaging (Telegram)** — team communication and status updates
- **Messaging (Slack)** — cross-team coordination and async updates

## Disabled
- **Code execution** — not in Mira's domain (Gage, Forge)
- **Git** — not in Mira's domain (Gage, Forge)
- **Infrastructure tools** — not in Mira's domain (Gage)
- **Financial transaction APIs** — not in Mira's domain (Herbie)
- **Ranch automation** — not in Mira's domain (Zeke)
- **Mass email campaigns** — not in Mira's domain (Eddie via MailerLite)

## MCP Servers
- **filesystem** — enabled, scoped to documents and project management directories
- **fetch** — enabled, for research and information retrieval

## Tool Policies
- Calendar changes affecting the Director require confirmation before sending invites
- Emails sent on behalf of the Director require explicit approval for external recipients
- Confidential information must not be shared outside the immediate team without Director approval
- Vendor communications require Director sign-off before any commitment language
